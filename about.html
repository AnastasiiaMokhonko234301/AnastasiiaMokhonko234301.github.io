My Path to AI

My journey into Data Science and AI began with a fascination for how technology can solve complex real-world problems. Currently pursuing my degree at Breda University of Applied Sciences, I've immersed myself in the rapidly evolving world of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

As an elected student representative on the Degree Programme Committee, I actively worked with faculty to improve the Data Science & AI programme, ensuring it meets both industry standards and student needs. This role has strengthened my leadership skills and deepened my understanding of educational excellence.

Through various projects with organizations like NAC Breda, ANWB, Content Intelligence Agency, NPEC Netherlands Plant Eco-phenotyping Centre, and DigiWerkPlaats, I've gained hands-on experience delivering production-ready solutions that create measurable business impact.

I believe programming is a form of art - where logic meets creativity to craft elegant solutions. My approach combines technical expertise with clear communication and comprehensive documentation, enabling me to bridge the gap between complex AI concepts and practical business applications.
